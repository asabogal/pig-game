PIG GAME!

The first player to reach 100 points on the global score, wins!

The round score accumulates per dice roll until the player holds the score

When the player holds the round score, this is added to the global score and the turn is passed to the next player

If the player rolls a 1, the round score resets to 0 and the turn is passed to the next player

Player 1 starts the first round
